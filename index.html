Leica's Classic French Yoghurt Cake Recipe

    Ingredients:
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • A pinch of salt
  1. Preheat your oven to 350¬∞F (175¬∞C). Grease and flour a cake pan.
  2. In a bowl, whisk the eggs and sugar until smooth.
  3. Add the yogurt, vegetable oil, lemon zest, and mix well.
  4. Sift the flour and baking powder into the mixture, add a pinch of salt, and fold until just combined.
  5. Pour the batter into the cake pan and bake for 30-35 minutes, or until a toothpick comes out clean.
  6. Let the cake cool before enjoying a slice of pure delight!
